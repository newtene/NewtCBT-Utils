# Privacy Policy for NewtCBT-Utils

**Last updated:** June 14, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.**
NewtCBT-Utils operates entirely locally within your browser. 

## 2. How We Use Permissions
The extension requests specific permissions (`tabs`, `storage`, and access to Google/YouTube/DuckDuckGo domains) strictly to provide its core functionality:
- **Tabs and Host Permissions**: Used to interact with Gemini and other pages directly in your browser.
- **Storage**: Used to temporarily store your query text locally to pass it between your current page and the AI tools. This data never leaves your device.
- **Declarative Net Request**: Used to cleanly manage network requests required for the extension's experience without routing traffic through any external servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The extension automates interactions with third-party websites like Google Gemini and YouTube on your behalf. Please review the privacy policies of those services, as their own data collection practices still apply when you use them. 

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
